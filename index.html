Welcome to MetaMars...

A courageous group of humans are leaving Earth behind to establish a literal “new world” on the red planet.
Terraforming has begun. There is no turning back.

MetaMars is a collection of “no-loss” arcade games that gives everyone a place to play for free, but rewards the best space cadets for their mastery of the harsh Martian landscape. Embrace your destiny and use your wits, speed, and competitive spirit to build our future utopia.

In-Game Economy and Monetary Colonization

From the peak of Olympus Mons, the tallest volcano in the Solar System, the reserve currency of MetaMars $OHM precipitates financial freedom for all MetaMartians.

The Olympus DAO has generously partnered with MetaMars to jumpstart the community's in-game economy and fuel the growth of our digital colony.

MetaMars is proud to be the first gaming platform to leverage Olympus as its reserve currency, and prior to the launch of the full experience, members of the community (holders of Mars Coins, see below) will be able to participate in OHM-backed gaming challenges in our private Discord.

Mars Coins

Mars Coins are the origin NFTs of the project, serving as the base community membership token and the foundation of the MetaMartian economy.

Mars Coins not only grant access past the airlock of our Discord, but also enable free eligibility to partake in the early plunders of terraforming MetaMars. While all players will be eligible to participate in the no-loss prize competitions, Mars Coin holders will be able to do so without any deposits required. More details can be found in our Discord community.

Three levels of Mars Coin below represent increasing scarcity and value.

  • Mars (900 supply)
  • Aqua (225 supply, 4x Mars)
  • Terra (50 supply, 20x Mars)
